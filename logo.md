# CivicOS Logo & Favicon Design Prompts

Use these prompts with Gemini (or any Image Gen AI) to design a premium, government-grade favicon and logo for CivicOS.

## Design Concept: "Digital Trust & Civic Duty"
The logo should represent the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) with a modern, technical, and approachable twist.

### Primary Logo Prompt (High-Fidelity)
> "A premium, minimalist professional logo for a government app called 'CivicOS'. The design features a stylized modern Ashoka Pillar wheel (Dharmachakra) integrated with a digital circuit pattern. Color palette: Deep Navy Blue (#002147) and Vibrant Cyan (#00F2FF). Glassmorphism style, 3D glossy finish, white background. Vector style, high-tech, trust-inspiring, 4k resolution."

### Favicon Specific Prompt (Simplified)
> "A simplified, high-contrast icon version of the CivicOS logo. A circular emblem featuring a bold 'C' integrated with a checkmark and a subtle digital node. Professional navy and cyan gradients. Flat design, pixel-perfect for 32x32, 64x64, and 128x128 sizes. Transparent background, sharp edges."
